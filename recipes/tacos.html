Tacos

Taco is a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a small hand-sized corn or wheat tortilla topped with a filling.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. 90% to 93% lean ground beef
  • 1 Tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce

Directions

  1. Add the beef to a large skillet over medium-high heat. Break the meat apart with a wooden spoon
  2. Reduce the heat to medium. Add the tomato sauce and water. Stir to combine.
